Arduino Workshop

The Arduino is a cheap, flexible, open source microcontroller platform designed to make it easy for hobbyists to use electronics in homemade projects. The Arduino offers you countless ways to create devices that interact with the world around you. The hands on workshop aims to teach you how to use an almost unlimited range of input and output add-ons, sensors,indicators, displays, motors, and more and also how the interfacing is done. Let's learn something new together.

Talk on Internet Of Things

Internet is no longer just network of networks of computers. Everything from Tesla cars, Amazon Echo, even your coffee pot could be connected to the Internet, thus called the Internet of Things.IOT drives the current consumer products market. It has the potential to exchange data, analyze and integrate it with other powerful technologies like AI and Big Data.Its the beginning of a new Era, so what are you waiting for?
